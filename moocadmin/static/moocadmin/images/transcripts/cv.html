High School: Crawford College Durban

South African National Senior Certificate with Exemption

I completed my high school education at Crawford College Durban (now called Eden College Durban) in Durban, South Africa. I earned a National Senior Certificate - the standard school leaving qualification in South Africa . This is awarded after 12 years of schooling, similar to English GCSE's.

I achieved 7 A's (80% +) and 1 B, with an average of 86.42%. Click below to view scans of the front and back of my National Senior Certificate.

University of KwaZulu-Natal

Qualification: BSc Computer Science and Mathematics (First Year)

I started my university education at the University of KwaZulu-Natal (UKZN) in January 2010. UKZN is a South African university located in Durban.

I achieved a year average of: 85.75% and 7/8 first class passes (a first class university pass in South African being 75%+). I also achieved two Deans Commendations for each semester, and two Certificates of Merit.

Deans Commendation - Awarded to students who perform 'exceptionally well', with an average of more than 75%. Click here for a full explanation and verification of this award from UKZN sources.

Certificate of Merit - One Certificate of Merit is awarded per module each semester to a single student. It is awarded for 'outstanding performance' to the 'best student' out of all students in a module. Click here for a full explanation and verification of this award from UKZN sources.
